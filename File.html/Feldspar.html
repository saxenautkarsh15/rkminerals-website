What it is ?

Feldspar is formed as a result of crystallization from magma veins in both intrusive and extrusive igneous rock and are also present in many types of metamorphic & sedimentary rocks. It is the most the abundant rock-forming mineral in nature and is basically aluminium silicate with potassium, sodium & calcium. It is generally marketed after grinding to specified a mesh according to consumer's needs.

Feldspars (KAlSi308 - NaA1Si308 - CaAl2Si208 ) are a group of rock-forming tectosilicateminerals that make up as much as 60% of the Earth's crust.

Uses of Feldspar

It is used in manufacturing glass pottery in high grade colourless glass.It is also used in ceramic industry as glazed in chinaware.
